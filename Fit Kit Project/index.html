FitKit

FitKit is a website designed to help people choose the right workouts based on their body type and fitness goals. Many beginners go to the gym without knowing what exercises they should focus on or how to structure a workout plan. FitKit helps solve this problem by explaining body types and giving simple workout suggestions that match different fitness goals.

On this site, users will learn about the three main body types and how each body type responds differently to exercise. Visitors will also find example workout routines designed for muscle gain, fat loss, and beginner fitness. The goal of FitKit is to make fitness easier to understand and help people build a workout routine that works for them.
